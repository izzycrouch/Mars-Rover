INFO:

 - Rovers navigate the surface of Mars by following a sequence of commands.

 - The surface of Mars is represented by a Plateau.

 - A rover's position on the Plateau will be represented by x and y co-ordinates and the letters N, S, W, E. 
   Which will represent the direction the rover is facing (North, South, West and East respectively).

 - The Rover takes a set of instructions to move on Mars.

    L: Rotates the Rover 90 degrees anti-clockwise.
    R: Rotates the Rover 90 degrees clockwise.
    M: Moves the Rover forward by one point.
